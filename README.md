# 🛡️ Squire VR
> **The definitive standalone sideloading ecosystem for Meta Quest.**

---

# ⚠️ PROJECT DISCONTINUED
### **Important Announcement regarding the future of Squire VR**

> **Squire VR is being discontinued.** After discussions with the **VRPirates** team, it’s clear that a native standalone app at this scale creates more 'heat' and infrastructure strain than they are comfortable with. Out of respect for the work they do to keep the mirrors alive, I am pulling the project to avoid compromising their ecosystem. Thanks for the 1.4k downloads in such a short time.

---

## 📈 Final Project Impact
In its short lifespan, Squire VR saw incredible traction. These metrics are a testament to the demand for native Quest utilities:

*   **Total Downloads:** `1,400+`
*   **Total Page Views:** `5,693`
*   **Growth Rate:** `+40% in 24 hours`
*   **Final Version:** `v2.4.0`

---

## 🛠️ Technical Retrospective
The development of Squire VR was a deep dive into **Native Android/Quest development**. While the distribution is ending, the project successfully demonstrated:

1.  **Direct Sideloading:** Bypassing the need for PC/Mobile bridges.
2.  **Optimized UI/UX:** A minimalist, high-performance interface for headset browsing.
3.  **Scalable Architecture:** Handling rapid user acquisition.
